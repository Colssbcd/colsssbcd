Welcome to Colonel's SSB Counselling Destination

Your dedicated partner in preparing for the Services Selection Board interviews. We provide expert guidance and personalized counselling to help you achieve your dream of joining the forces.

Our Mission

Our program is designed to cultivate in aspirants of the Armed Forces the essential "Officer Like Qualities" (OLQs) required for selection. Through focused training, we refine their presentation and enhance their alignment with the expectations of the Services, thereby maximizing their potential for recommendation.

Beyond military aspirations, our training fosters the development of well-rounded citizens who benefit from comprehensive personality enhancement. Participants will cultivate leadership traits applicable across diverse professional environments. Recognizing the offline nature of the Armed Forces selection process, we prioritize in-person instruction to provide authentic, firsthand experience under the guidance of seasoned Assessors and Mentors.

Our Courses

I. Comprehensive Offline Course (Two Weeks)

  • Screening Test Preparation: Focused training on Officer Intelligence Rating (OIR) tests and Picture Perception & Discussion Test (PPDT).
  • Psychological Assessment Training: In-depth practice and guidance on Thematic Apperception Test (TAT), Word Association Test (WAT), Situation Reaction Test (SRT), and Self-Description (SD).
  • Group Testing Officer (GTO) Tasks: Practical, real-time execution of all GTO tasks in a dedicated outdoor arena under expert supervision.
  • Interview Preparation: Development of interview techniques and personalized one-on-one mock interviews with feedback.
  • Performance Enhancement: Guided sessions on meditation and concentration techniques for improved focus and results.
  • Experiential Activities: Includes activities such as firing practice (subject to conditions).
  • Logistics: Boarding and lodging facilities are available upon request.

II. Structured Online Course (Two Weeks)

  • Daily Sessions: Two hours of interactive learning each evening (8:00 PM - 10:00 PM IST).
  • Screening Test Guidance: Covering OIR and PPDT essentials.
  • Psychological Assessment Training: Comprehensive guidance on TAT, WAT, SRT, and SD.
  • GTO Task Fundamentals: Understanding the approach and methodology for attempting GTO tasks effectively.
  • Interview Preparation: Key strategies and preparation techniques for the personal interview.

III. Intensive Mock SSB (Six Days)

  • Full SSB Simulation: A complete six-day mock SSB process conducted under the observation of expert assessors.
  • Targeted Feedback: Provides detailed performance analysis and specific recommendations for improvement.
  • Ideal for Repeaters: Especially beneficial for candidates who have previously attended SSB and require focused refinement.
